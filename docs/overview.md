# Project Overview: Medgel Next.js

## Introduction

Medgel is a modern, full-stack web application designed for **Medgel Pvt. Ltd.**, a leading pharmaceutical company specializing in the manufacturing of Soft Gel Capsules. The platform serves as both a corporate showcase and a Content Management System (CMS), allowing administrators to manage products, news, job openings, and site content dynamically.

## Core Features

### 1. Corporate Showcase

- **Home Page**: Features a dynamic slider, key highlights (CardRow), and corporate purpose sections.
- **About Us**: Information about the company, its history, and awards.
- **Facilities**: Detailed view of the manufacturing facilities, including state-of-the-art drying technology.
- **Products**: A comprehensive product catalog with categorization (Generics, Oncology, Respiratory, etc.).
- **News & Updates**: Latest company news and industry updates.
- **Investor Relations**: Access to annual reports and corporate policies.

### 2. Careers & Job Applications

- **Job Openings**: List of current vacancies within the company.
- **Online Application**: Potential candidates can apply directly through the website and upload their resumes.

### 3. Dynamic Content Management (Admin)

- **Inline Editing**: Administrators can edit specific sections of the website directly from the frontend using a custom CMS interface.
- **Image Uploads**: Integrated image management for banners, products, and news.
- **Data Management**: Admin panels for managing product lists, categories, and user inquiries.

### 4. Interactive Forms

- **Contact Us**: Professional contact forms for business inquiries.
- **Email Notifications**: Automated email alerts for form submissions using Nodemailer.

## Target Audience

- **B2B Partners**: Pharmaceutical companies looking for manufacturing partners.
- **Investors**: Individuals or entities interested in the company's performance and policies.
- **Job Seekers**: Professionals looking for career opportunities in the pharmaceutical industry.
- **General Public**: Users seeking information about Medgel's products and initiatives.

## Goal

The primary goal of this project is to provide Medgel with a robust, scalable, and easy-to-manage web presence that reflects its position as a leader in the pharmaceutical manufacturing space.
